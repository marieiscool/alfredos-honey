Alfredo Neuman tends to more than 100 hives located on farms in Western Henrico and Goochland county.

Originally from Barranquilla, Colombia Alfredo started Beekeeping over 20 years ago as a tribute to his brother who was also a renowned Beekeeper in his native country.
